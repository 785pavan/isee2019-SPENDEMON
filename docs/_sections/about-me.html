How many times have each of us faced the end of the month with a bewildered expression asking where on earth did we spend all our money on? I would say many times. And whenever we face such a situation, how many times have we created an Excel sheet or downloaded a complicated App to keep track of all the expenses but gave up because it was too much work? If your answer again is many times, I think our App SPENDEMON is something you need in your life.
