How many times have each of us faced the end of the month with a bewildered expression asking where on earth did we spend all our money on? I would say many times. And whenever we face such a situation, how many times have we created an Excel sheet or downloaded a complicated App to keep track of all the expenses but gave up because it was too much work? If your answer again is many times, I think our App SPENDEMON is something you need in your life. SPENDEMON is an App tailor-made by lazy-ass students for lazy-ass people, who do not want overcomplicated App features and simply want a simple and effective User Interface such that they can record and view their transactions with a tap and a glance. So if you are interested in making your lives a bit more simpler and are kind enough to give a try to a sincere effort by some newbie App Developers, just click here
